❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check in as a visitor? ▼
Simply scan the QR code at any campus entrance or reception area using your smartphone. This will open the check-in form. Fill in your details, select your visit reason and department, then submit. You'll receive instant confirmation!
What information do I need to provide? ▼
Required information includes: Full Name, Email Address, Reason for Visit, and Host/Person to Meet. Optional information includes: Phone Number, Company/Organization, Department, and Additional Notes. This helps us ensure your smooth visit to campus.
How long does check-in take? ▼
The entire check-in process typically takes less than 2 minutes. It's a quick and seamless experience designed to minimize wait times at campus entrances.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes, your data is stored securely in your browser's local storage. Jinja College takes data protection seriously and follows strict security protocols. Your information is only used for campus security and administrative purposes.
What if I don't have a smartphone? ▼
If you don't have a smartphone, please visit the main reception desk where staff can help you complete the check-in process manually. We're happy to assist all visitors!
Can I use the system on a tablet or computer? ▼
Absolutely! Our system is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and computers. Simply visit the check-in URL or scan the QR code from any device.
What are visit reasons available? ▼
Available visit reasons include: Meeting, Delivery, Interview, Facility Tour, Academic Purpose, Event/Conference, and Other. Select the option that best describes your visit.
How will the admin be notified? ▼
Administrators receive real-time notifications through the system when you check in. Depending on settings, they may also receive email notifications or browser pop-up alerts to ensure a smooth welcome experience.
What if I encounter an error during check-in? ▼
Common errors can usually be resolved by: 1) Ensuring all required fields are filled, 2) Checking your internet connection, 3) Refreshing the page, 4) Clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, contact the reception desk for assistance.

🔧 Troubleshooting

QR Code Not Scanning?

Make sure your camera is clean and well-lit. Try from different angles. Ensure your phone's camera has focus. If still having issues, manually visit the check-in URL.

Form Won't Submit?

Check that all required fields (marked with *) are completed. Verify your email address is correct. Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Try clearing your browser cache.

Network/Connectivity Issues?

Connect to the campus WiFi network. If problem persists, try switching to cellular data. Check your router/WiFi status. Contact IT support if connectivity issues continue.

Browser Compatibility?

Our system works best with modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. Update your browser to the latest version if you experience issues.
